eSender - Virtual Phone Number SMS Service

2025-04-29 / eSender  
업데이트 : --01

eSender is a virtual phone number service that provides Chinese and Hong Kong phone numbers through WeChat. Here are the key points about eSender:

  • eSender : www.esenderchina.com

1. Purpose:

  • It allows users to receive SMS verification codes and messages from Chinese/Hong Kong services without needing a physical SIM card
  • Particularly useful for people who need to register for Chinese services (like financial services, e-shopping, etc.) that require a Chinese phone number for verification

2. Key Features:

  • Works through WeChat official account
  • Provides virtual phone numbers for both China and Hong Kong
  • Allows unlimited SMS reception
  • No need to change physical SIM cards
  • Supports sending SMS for registration on Chinese websites
  • Real-name registration is required (following telecom regulations)

3. How it works:

  • Users follow the eSender WeChat official account
  • Register with required information (including passport details for real-name verification)
  • Receive a virtual phone number
  • Can receive SMS messages directly through WeChat

4. Pricing:

  • Offers various subscription periods (30 days, 90 days, 180 days, 360 days)
  • China number prices start from 28 HKD (approximately 3.60 USD) for 30 days
  • Hong Kong number prices start from 18 HKD (approximately 2.30 USD) for 30 days

eSender is particularly useful for international users who need to access Chinese services that require local phone numbers for verification purposes, without having to physically obtain a Chinese SIM card.
