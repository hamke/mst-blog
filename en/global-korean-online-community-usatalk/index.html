How to Advertise on the USATalk Korean Community Website

2025-05-01 / USATalk  
Updated on : --01

USATalk is an online community for Koreans living in or interested in the United States, as well as foreigners who speak Korean.

It is a space for sharing useful information and experiences about life in the U.S., primarily used by Korean students, expatriates, travelers, and immigrants currently living in the country.

  • Visit USATalk: https://www.usatalk.net/

Key Features of USATalk

  1. Sharing Information About Life in the U.S.

    • Visas, banking, mobile phone setup, transportation, etc.
    • Well-organized tips and guides for newcomers

  2. Region-Based Community

    • Sub-groups and boards for major cities like New York, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Seattle, Washington DC, Chicago, etc.
    • Active sharing of local info and meetups

  3. Study Abroad & Working Holiday Info

    • U.S. university admissions, language programs, exchange programs, scholarships
    • Active student discussions and working holiday experiences

  4. Marketplace & Job Board

    • Job postings from Korean businesses, restaurants, cafés, academies, etc.
    • Buy/sell secondhand electronics, furniture, rooms, attraction tickets, and more

  5. Culture & Community Activities

    • Korean food, cultural events, K-culture news
    • Stories about dating/marrying Americans and navigating cultural differences

How to Advertise on USATalk

Most visitors to USATalk are Koreans or Korean speakers living in the U.S., either short-term or long-term.
If your product or service is related to the U.S., you can run highly targeted ads toward this audience.

In addition, ad posts are SEO-optimized for effective exposure on Google, Naver, and other search engines.

  • USATalk Advertising Info: https://www.usatalk.net/advertising/

For inquiries, contact us via email ( hello@globaltalk.asia ) or KakaoTalk ( www.usatalk.net/kakao/ ).
