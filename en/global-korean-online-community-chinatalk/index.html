How to Advertise on the ChinaTalk Korean Community Website

2025-05-01 / ChinaTalk  
Updated on : --01

ChinaTalk is an online community for Koreans living in or interested in China, as well as foreigners who speak Korean.

It’s a place to share useful information and experiences about life in China, actively used by Korean students, expatriates, travelers, and immigrants currently residing in China.

  • Visit ChinaTalk: https://www.chinatalk.net/

Key Features of ChinaTalk

  1. Sharing Information About Life in China

    • Visa, banking, mobile phone setup, transportation tips, etc.
    • Well-organized guides for those settling in China for the first time

  2. Region-Based Community

    • Sub-groups and boards for major cities like Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Hangzhou, etc.
    • Active sharing of local information and meetups

  3. Study Abroad & Working Holiday Info

    • Info about Chinese universities, language programs, exchange programs, scholarships, etc.
    • Active discussions among students, including working holiday experiences

  4. Marketplace & Job Board

    • Job postings from Korean companies, restaurants, cafes, academies, etc.
    • Buy/sell second-hand electronics, furniture, rooms, attraction tickets, and more

  5. Culture & Community Activities

    • Korean food, cultural events, and K-culture news
    • Discussions about dating/marriage with Chinese partners and cultural differences

How to Advertise on ChinaTalk

Most visitors to ChinaTalk are Koreans or Korean speakers living in China, either short-term or long-term.
If your product or service is related to China, you can run targeted ads aimed at this audience.

In addition, all ad posts are SEO-optimized to be effectively exposed to search engines like Google and Naver.

  • ChinaTalk Advertising Information: https://www.chinatalk.net/advertising/

For inquiries about advertising, please contact us via email ( hello@chinatalk.net ) or KakaoTalk ( www.chinatalk.net/kakao/ ).
