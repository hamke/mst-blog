How to Check and Download Your KakaoPay QR Code (for Receiving)

2025-05-06 / KakaoPay  
Updated on : --01

MoneySkillTalk introduces how to check and download your KakaoPay receiving QR code.

First, open the KakaoTalk mobile app, then go to the “More” menu and tap the “Scan” button at the top.

When the “Scan” screen appears, tap the “Receive” option at the bottom.

Once you tap “Receive,” your personal KakaoPay receiving QR code will be displayed.

Entering an amount is optional. If you enter an amount, a QR code with a fixed amount will be generated. If you leave it blank, a QR code allowing the sender to freely enter the amount will be automatically created.

To download the QR code image, tap the download icon below the code.

The KakaoPay Mobile Transfer Link Generator is an online service that allows you to easily generate a mobile transfer link using the downloaded QR code (for receiving).

By placing the generated link on your website or blog, customers can send payments to your KakaoPay account with a single click—no extra steps required.
